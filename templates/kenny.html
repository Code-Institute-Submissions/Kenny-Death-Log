Everyone's favourite poor kid. Cartman has sung a song about his poverty, rats have eaten his corpse, he has been run over and blown up. Yes, he's the reason this site exists and may just be everyone's guilty pleasure in South Park. It's impossible not to feel sorry for Kenny, with his constant misfortune and numerous demises being the focal point of the show. As much as life beats him down (and Cthulhu reincarnates him), Kenny has proven to have a heart of gold, sacrificing himself more than once to save his friends, as well as toiling as part of a child labour force to buy his younger sister a dolly to play with because their deadbeat parents can't.

Kenny died permanently in season 5 as a result of terminal illness. This seems to be the only time the boys recognised that he had actually died and his departure affects the group immensely. It's not known whether Kenny's death was supposed to be permanent, but he was brought back as a soul trapped in Cartman's body, before being reincarnated and featuring in the show once again. It was after this point that his regular deaths all but ended, as the creators felt that the gag had run dry.
