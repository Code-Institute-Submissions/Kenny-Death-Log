Greatest Quotes:

Stan is one of the only voices of reason in South Park, with a wisdom that seems beyond his years. His mannerisms reflect this, as he is often seen walking with his hands in his pockets, deep in thought. He has also experienced clinical depression as a result of seeing the world from an adult perspective when he reached the age of 10. Besides this, he is the natural leader of the group when Cartman hasn't led them into yet another scheme to wreck havok on South Park.

Stan's strong bond with Kyle has been prevalent since the first season and they have remained the very best of friends since then, much like their fathers, Randy and Gerald. Speaking of embarrassing parents, Randy has evolved to become one of the shows funniest characters, constantly getting himself and others into hair-brained situations, much to Stan's chagrin.
