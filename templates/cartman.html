Eric Cartman. The face of South Park and perhaps one of the greatest villains TV has ever seen. His constant disdain for anything different has brought laughs to fans for twenty years. Whether it's the Jews, blacks, hippies, women, gays or Canadians, you can always trust Cartman to be on-hand with witty putdowns and gasp-inducing remarks. He is the essence of anti-PC and the biggest tool in South Park's arsenal to offend and delight in equal measure.

As the series has evolved, Cartman has come to show a tremendous ability to manipulate and scheme, reading his opponent's moves well in advance. There's no doubt he actually possesses a great intellect and capacity to adapt to any given situation in an instant, something which his friends and lifelong rival, Kyle, must be aware of, but choose to remain oblivious.
